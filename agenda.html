March 5 2026 — 20:15 @ De Doelen, Rotterdam

"Vorex" will be performed by Postland. The work is part of "Sound of View", a collaboration between filmmaker Barbara Meter, four composers, and the ensemble, in which one film is presented four times with four different newly composed live soundtracks.

March 14 2026 — 15:30 @ Orgelpark, Amsterdam

As part of Amsterdam Marimba Weekend, "Workshop and Artists' Talks" will take place ahead of the evening premiere of "Fidellica". The session addresses collaboration between performers, composers, and programmers, with emphasis on developing new artistic work.
- Event page

March 14 2026 — 20:15 @ Orgelpark, Amsterdam

"Fidellica", a new work for solo marimba written for Rachel Xi Zhang, will be premiered during Amsterdam Marimba Weekend. The piece is part of the "Dowland Series" and is based on John Dowland's "Go Crystal Tears".
- Event page

"Vorex" will be performed by Postland as part of "Sound of View", the collaborative film project with four distinct live soundtracks.

May 16 2026 — 20:15 @ Orgelpark, Amsterdam

Two multimedia works, "Velarium" for harpsichord, electronics and real-time visuals and "NoiseSample" for live electronics and real-time visuals, will be performed in a concert presented in collaboration with harpsichordist Liubov Titarenko. The event is part of Nieuwe Noten Amsterdam, and the program brings together early and contemporary repertoire.
- Event page

September 11 2026 @ Theater Kikker, Utrecht

A new multimedia work for Orkest de Ereprijs will be premiered during Gaudeamus Festival. The work features trombonist Peter de Hoop, with electronics and real-time visuals. The composition is co-commissioned by Gaudeamus and Orkest de Ereprijs.
