Yu Oda

Composer and Multimedia Artist

Yu Oda is a composer and multimedia artist based in the Netherlands. His work focuses on audio reactive visuals, real time graphics, live electronics, and contemporary composition.

His practice integrates Max/MSP/Jitter, WebGL, and custom software with acoust music composition for performance, installation, and digital media projects.
