Yu Oda

Composer and Multimedia Artist – Netherlands

Yu Oda is a Netherlands-based composer and multimedia artist specializing in electroacoustic music, modular synthesizers, live electronics, and interactive audiovisual projects.

His work integrates Max/MSP/Jitter, WebGL, real-time graphics, audio-reactive visuals, and custom software for performances, installations, and digital media.
